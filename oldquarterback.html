Eli is too Old!

Eli Manning has been a great quarterback for the New York Giants for a long time, but he is now 37 years old. He arguably the greatest quarterback in the history of the Giants franchise, but his stats have gone down significantly in the last 3 years. He is getting too old to continue and this is becoming one of the Giants main concerns.
